About MCM

MCM (Mathematical Contest in Modeling) is an international mathematical modeling competition that encourages students to use mathematical methods to solve real-world problems. Teams are required to complete modeling, solving, and paper writing for a real problem within a specified time.

This website aims to provide visualization-related materials for the 2026 MCM Contest Problem C, including problem presentation, solution approaches, paper summaries, and typical cases, to help everyone better prepare for and participate in the competition.
